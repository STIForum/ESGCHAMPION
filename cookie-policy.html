Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how STIF ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit the ESG Champions Platform.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Category Purpose Duration
Essential Required for authentication, security, and basic platform functionality Session / 7 days
Functional Remember your preferences and settings 1 year
Analytics Help us understand how visitors use the platform 2 years

3. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Authentication tokens: Keep you logged in securely
  • Session cookies: Maintain your session state
  • Security cookies: Protect against CSRF and other attacks

4. Functional Cookies

These enhance your experience by remembering choices you make:

  • Language preferences
  • Theme settings (light/dark mode)
  • Last visited pages for "Continue where you left off"

5. Analytics Cookies

We use analytics to understand how visitors interact with our platform. This data is aggregated and anonymous. We may use:

  • Page view tracking
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Performance monitoring

6. Third-Party Cookies

Our platform uses services that may set their own cookies:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database services
  • LinkedIn: If you choose to sign in with LinkedIn

7. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Note: Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features of the platform.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at: privacy@stif.org
