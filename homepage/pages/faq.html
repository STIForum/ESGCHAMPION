STIF is a mission-led social venture that turns complex ESG standards into practical tools and frameworks so small and mid-sized businesses can grow responsibly and report with confidence.
Unlike generic ESG tools, STIF is co-created with practitioners and tailored for SMEs, prioritising practical disclosure, realistic expectations, and step-by-step improvement rather than just scores.
STIF operates on a sustainable business model through subscriptions, premium tools, and advisory services for businesses, while reinvesting in framework development, community activities, and continuous product improvement.

You can get started by requesting early access or booking a call with the team, then selecting the tools and level of support that best fit your organisation's size, sector, and sustainability goals.
ESG Champions are sustainability professionals, consultants, and practitioners who contribute their expertise to validate, adapt, and enhance STIF's ESG frameworks for real-world use by SMEs.
As a Champion, you help shape practical ESG standards for smaller businesses, extend your professional impact beyond individual clients, and join a community focused on turning sustainability ambition into action.
Champions participate in review cycles, working groups, and feedback sessions, stress-testing indicators and disclosures to ensure they are clear, achievable, and aligned with both global standards and everyday operations.
Our ESG Champion ranking tool enables Champions to be recognised as contributors to specific frameworks, invited to thought-leadership opportunities, and featured in selected community content, depending on their level of participation.
No fixed hours are required. Champions can contribute flexibly based on availability, choosing when to join reviews, pilots, or discussions that match their interests and expertise.
STIF is built primarily for small and mid-sized businesses that want to strengthen their sustainability performance and reporting without the complexity or cost of enterprise-level ESG solutions.
