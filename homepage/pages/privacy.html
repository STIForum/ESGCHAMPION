STIF Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

GREENURBS INNOVATION LTD (trading as "STIF", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform and services. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the STIF platform, including Business Users, ESG Champions, and website visitors. Data Controller: GREENURBS INNOVATION LTD Company Number: 14451676 Registered Office: C/O The Accountancy Partnership Suite 5, 5th Floor, City Reach, Greenwich View Place, London, England, E14 9NN

2. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. Our legal bases include: • Contract performance: Processing necessary to perform our contract with you • Legitimate interests: Processing necessary for our legitimate business interests • Consent: Where you have given specific consent for processing • Legal obligation: Processing necessary to comply with legal requirements

3. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly: • Account Registration: Name, email address, job title, company name, contact details • Profile Information: Professional background, areas of expertise • Business Information: Company size, sector, sustainability goals, ESG data • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details • Communications: Messages, feedback, support requests Information Collected Automatically: • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform • Device Data: IP address, browser type, operating system • Cookies and similar technologies (see our Cookie Policy)

4. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision: Creating and managing your Account, providing platform access, processing transactions, delivering support. Service Improvement: Analysing usage patterns, developing new features, conducting research. Communication: Sending administrative information, providing updates, marketing communications (with consent). Legal and Security: Enforcing our Terms, protecting against fraud, complying with legal obligations.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share your information only with: • Service Providers bound by data processing agreements • In business transfers (mergers, acquisitions) • When required by law • To protect our rights, property, or safety • With your explicit consent

6. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored within the United Kingdom and EEA. If we transfer data outside the UK/EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the ICO.

7. Data Retention

• Account Data: Retained while active and for up to 6 months after closure • Financial Records: Retained for 7 years for UK tax requirements • Marketing Data: Retained until consent is withdrawn or 2 years of inactivity • Usage Analytics: Retained in aggregated, anonymised form indefinitely

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures including encryption, regular security assessments, access controls, employee training, and incident response procedures. No method of transmission is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the right to: • Access a copy of your personal data • Rectification of inaccurate data • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") • Restrict processing • Data portability • Object to processing • Withdraw consent • Lodge a complaint with the ICO To exercise these rights, contact us at https://www.stif.org.

10. Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in any marketing email, updating your preferences, or contacting https://www.stif.org.

11. Children's Privacy

The STIF platform is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy, sending email notification, and displaying a prominent notice.
