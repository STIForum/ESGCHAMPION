About STIF

Sustainability Technology and Innovation Forum

We're building a global community of sustainability experts dedicated to validating and improving ESG practices across industries.

Our Mission

Driving Sustainable Change Through Expertise

The ESG Champions Platform was created to harness the collective expertise of sustainability professionals worldwide. We believe that meaningful change happens when experts collaborate to validate, refine, and improve ESG indicators.

Our platform enables professionals from diverse backgrounds to contribute their knowledge, review ESG metrics, and help organizations make better sustainability decisions.

2024

Founded with a vision to democratize ESG expertise and make sustainability accessible to all.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of accuracy and honesty in all ESG validations.

Collaboration

We bring together experts from around the world to share knowledge and insights.

Impact

Every contribution on our platform drives real-world sustainability improvements.
