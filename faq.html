Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the ESG Champions Platform.

General Questions

What is the ESG Champions Platform?

The ESG Champions Platform is a collaborative community where sustainability experts can validate, review, and improve ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) indicators. Champions contribute their expertise to ensure accurate and meaningful sustainability metrics.

Who can become an ESG Champion?

Anyone with expertise or interest in sustainability, ESG practices, or related fields can join. We welcome professionals from diverse backgrounds including corporate sustainability, consulting, research, finance, and non-profit sectors.

Is the platform free to use?

Yes, registration and participation on the ESG Champions Platform is completely free. We believe in open access to sustainability knowledge and encourage broad participation.

Participation & Reviews

How do I submit a review?

After logging in, navigate to the Panels section, select a panel that matches your expertise, choose an indicator, and click "Submit Review." Provide your detailed analysis and rating, then submit for review by our moderators.

How are reviews evaluated?

Reviews are evaluated by our admin team based on quality, accuracy, and relevance. Approved reviews earn credits, and high-quality contributions may receive additional recognition. Community members can also upvote helpful reviews.

What are ESG Panels?

ESG Panels are thematic categories covering different aspects of Environmental, Social, and Governance practices. Examples include Climate Action, Human Rights, and Corporate Governance. Each panel contains specific indicators that Champions can review.

Credits & Rankings

How do credits work?

Credits are awarded when your reviews are approved by moderators. The standard award is 10 credits per accepted review. Additional credits can be earned through community engagement such as receiving upvotes on your contributions.

What is the STIF Score?

Your STIF Score is a composite metric that reflects your overall contribution to the platform. It includes credits from approved reviews, community engagement (votes received), and participation frequency. Higher scores improve your ranking on the leaderboard.

How does the leaderboard work?

The leaderboard ranks Champions based on their total credits. We feature both all-time rankings and periodic rankings (weekly, monthly) to recognize consistent contributors. Top Champions may receive special recognition and badges.

Technical Questions

Can I use LinkedIn to sign in?

Yes, you can sign in with your LinkedIn account for a seamless experience. This also helps verify your professional credentials and makes it easier to connect with other sustainability professionals on the platform.

Is my data secure?

We take data security seriously. The platform uses industry-standard encryption, secure authentication via Supabase, and follows best practices for data protection. Please review our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

For Businesses & SMEs

What is STIF sustainability reporting for businesses?

STIF provides SMEs with an accessible sustainability reporting platform tailored to their size and industry. Our system simplifies complex ESG frameworks (GRI, ESRS, IFRS) into manageable indicators, helping businesses track, measure, and report on their environmental, social, and governance performance.

How do I get started with sustainability reporting?

After registering a business account, complete your company profile and select the ESG frameworks relevant to your industry. Our platform will recommend indicators based on your sector and help you collect data step-by-step. You can start with basic metrics and expand as your reporting matures.

What ESG frameworks does STIF support?

STIF currently supports GRI (Global Reporting Initiative), ESRS (European Sustainability Reporting Standards), and IFRS Sustainability Disclosure Standards. We continuously update our indicator library as frameworks evolve and new standards emerge.

How much does the business subscription cost?

We offer flexible pricing plans designed for SMEs. Contact our team for current pricing information or schedule a demo to learn which plan fits your business needs. We also offer discounts for annual subscriptions and non-profit organizations.

Can I export my sustainability data?

Yes, all your sustainability data can be exported in various formats including CSV and PDF reports. This allows you to share reports with stakeholders, integrate with other systems, or maintain offline records of your ESG performance.

How do the indicators relate to my industry?

When you set up your profile, you select your industry and business focus areas. STIF then recommends the most material and relevant indicators for your sector. Our ESG Champions community also provides expert insights on which indicators matter most for different industries.
